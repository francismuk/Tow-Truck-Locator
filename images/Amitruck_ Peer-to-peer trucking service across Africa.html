How it works

1.

Book a delivery for your load using our Mobile app (Website coming soon)

2.

The nearest trucks are informed and their quotes are sent to you on the app.

3.

Choose a quote and make a payment

4.

Payment is held in escrow and the vehicle is dispatched to make your delivery

5.

You may now contact driver for updates

6.

You can now track your shipment live on the app

7.

Once you confirm delivery has been made successfully on the App payment is released to the driver

Drive for us

Amitruck improves drivers work by enabling them to access more clients, plan their days ahead, reduce idle time and empty runs. It ensures transparency, accurate trip information and fair prompt payment.

How it works

  • Receive delivery requests directly on the APP
  • You provide a quote (for jobs you want to do)
  • Pick up and deliver the goods
  • Prompt payment is made once delivery is confirmed

Driver benefits

  • Full control of your vehicle
  • Choose when & what work you do
  • Accurate distance
  • You set the price
  • Prompt payment
